Clementine was adopted by her forever-family in early 2017. It took her a while to warm up to her Mom, but the love she had with her Dad was almost instant. Once she felt confortable in her new home and family she started putting on a bit of weight and started looking more like a cat, and less like a kitten. Her young-at-heart spirit stayed and she taught herself how to play fetch with small foam balls.

Her favorite color is pink, and favorite thing to do is lay outside the warm sidewalk. She consider's her Mom's home office her bedroom and likes to play favorites when she always chooses her Dad's lap and chest to cuddle with on the couch. Unlike some cats, she likes to be held, get kisses, and prefers to be the center of attention whenever possible, which can sometimes be especially hard when her sister, Kiwi is around.
